Instructions

Objective

The objective of the game is to escape the evil humans' home To do this, you must play as the dog, and battle your way out. First, you face off against the evil baby... DO NOT be fooled by his size. Then, if you manage to defeat him, you may move on to face the big, bad boss... The Dad.

Controls

Movement: Up Arrow: UP Down Arrow: DOWN Right Arrow: RIGHT Left Arrow: LEFT Attack: A list of attacks will appear onscreen, and you must chose one to attack the foe.
